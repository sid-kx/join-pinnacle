Commission Structure

A Simple, Agent-First Model

At Pinnacle, our commission structure is designed to give agents more control over their earnings while still providing access to the systems, support, and tools needed to operate at a high level.

100% Commission Structure

Our agents keep 100% of their commission and pay only a transaction fee that helps cover the professional tools, systems, and resources we provide to support their business.

This model is built for driven professionals who want the freedom to grow their business with strong brokerage backing, modern infrastructure, and a clear path to long-term success.
